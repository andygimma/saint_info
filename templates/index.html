Sante Info

Description

We are building an IVR (Interactive Voice Response) powered database which will help in prevention of the disease by:

1. Providing information to the general public on the disease and how to prevent infection

2. Providing a crowd-sourced, open-source database of instances of the disease as they occur in real-time

3. By helping health-care providers better direct and co-ordinate their response to the disease while also helping them connect to the public to spread information on how to prevent spreading the disease
